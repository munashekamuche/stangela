Our Story

Educating Minds, Inspiring Hearts

Founded in 1998, St Angela Academy began as a small day school in Harare with a vision to provide world-class Cambridge education to Zimbabwean students. Over the years, we have grown into a premier educational institution with two campuses, serving over 800 students and maintaining our commitment to academic excellence and character development.
