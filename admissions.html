Begin Your Academic Journey Today

Join St Angela Academy and become part of Zimbabwe's premier Cambridge curriculum school. We welcome applications from students seeking academic excellence, character development, and preparation for global opportunities. Our admissions process is designed to identify students who will thrive in our supportive learning environment.

How to Apply

1

Submit Application

Complete our online application form with your academic records, personal information, and supporting documents.

2

Send Documents

Submit required documents including previous school reports, birth certificate, and passport photos to complete your application.

3

Interview Process

Meet with our admissions team for an interview to discuss your academic goals and ensure St Angela Academy is the right fit for you.

4

Receive Decision

Receive your admission decision and enrollment information. Successful applicants will receive details about orientation and the start of their academic journey.

Academic Records

Recommendation Letters
